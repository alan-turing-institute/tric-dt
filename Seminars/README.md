# 'Crafting Digital Twins' Seminar Series

By focusing on the methodological hurdles of the foundational work, this series will offer immediate value to researchers and developers, enabling everyone to acquire practical insights applicable to their project at any stage, no matter how â€œDT-readyâ€. Each Seminar will follow roughly this format:
- Total of 1,5 hour duration
- 10 minutes of welcome & TRIC-DT announcements
- 2 x 15 - 20 minute talks (from different themes)
- Plenty of room for Q&A and discussions
